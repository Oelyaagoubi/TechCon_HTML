TechCon began in 1987 as a modest gathering of 200 technology enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. Its founders, a group of engineers, envisioned a space where emerging technologies, particularly microcomputers, could be discussed and developed. The inaugural event, though small, marked the beginning of what would become a major global technology conference.

TechCon provides a platform for breakthrough technologies and creative ideas, encouraging innovation across fields.it connects professionals to collaborate and build partnerships, driving advancements together

Tim Berners-Lee, the British computer scientist who created the World Wide Web in 1989, was a keynote speaker at TechCon 2005. His invention revolutionized how information is shared and accessed globally. At TechCon, Berners-Lee spoke about the importance of open standards and his vision for the future of the internet.
